What's new in the Game Boy Advance version of Donkey Kong Country?

Plenty. Donkey Kong Country is now better than ever thanks to some great special features exclusive to the GBA version of the game. Funky's Fishing is a new mini-game which challenges you to hook as many fish as you can within a time limit, and Candy's Dance Studio will test your rhythm with fun dance games. Either the main adventure or these new mini-games can be played with one or two players!

Other additions include collectable character art (earned by finding hidden cameras throughout the game) and a detailed statistics tracker. A new DK Attack mode ranks your performance on completed levels based on a number of factors. To get the best score, you'll have to exhibit near-perfect skill on each level.

Where is a good spot to get lots of free lives?

Jungle Hijinx is a great spot to build up extra lives. First, finish the level. Then re-enter the level and work your way back to the Continue Barrel. Next, go to the right and collect the golden Expresso Animal Token. Press Start and exit the level. Re-enter the level and get the Animal Token again. Repeat this until you get three animal tokens and you'll go to the Expresso bonus level. In the bonus level you'll be able to pick up at least 5 or 6 extra lives every time!

How do I get items that are floating in mid-air?

Do the Barrel Roll off a ledge, then jump while you're still rolling to gain some extra distance.

How do I go back to worlds that I've already finished?

Talk to Funky Kong to take one of Funky's Flights to a previous area.
